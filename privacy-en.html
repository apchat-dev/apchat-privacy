Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 16, 2025

APChat (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the APChat mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly from users. All accounts are created and managed by authorized administrators. During account creation, we may store limited information such as:

  • Phone number (for account identification and login)
  • User name and profile details provided by the administrator

2. How We Use Information

The information stored in APChat is used solely to provide secure access to the chat platform and enable communication features within the App.

3. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to deliver push notifications related to chat activity and system updates. Firebase may collect non-personal usage data to ensure reliable message delivery.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties. Access to user data is limited to authorized administrators within the organization.

5. Data Storage and Security

All user data is stored securely on servers managed by our organization. We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Data Retention

User information will be retained only for as long as necessary for internal communication purposes or as required by applicable laws.

7. Your Rights

Since accounts are created by administrators, users who wish to update, correct, or delete their information may contact their administrator for assistance.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the updated revision date.
